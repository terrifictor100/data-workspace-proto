HMRC Data

Statistics that record the movement of trade goods between the UK and both EU and non-EU countries.

Import and export data for EU countries are collected via a survey of VAT-registered businesses, known as Intrastat. Data for non-EU countries are collected from customs declarations made to HMRC.

Current uses for this data include production of the department's Core Statistics Book and country fact sheets, and internal analysis such as trade by product code and tariff analysis.

Available data sets

UK exporters

Basic data of businesses that have exported goods to non-EU countries.

UK exports to EU countries

Data of goods exported from UK to EU countries.

UK exports to non-EU countries

Data of goods exported from UK to non-EU countries.

UK importers

Basic data of businesses that have imported goods from non-EU countries.

UK imports from EU countries

Data of goods imported from EU countries to the UK.

UK imports from non-EU countries

Data collected by HMRC of goods imported from non-EU countries to the UK.
